ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Szymon Krzysztofik, I'm a 21-year-old artist from Poland. I draw whenever I can, but since I'm currently studying, I don't have as much time for it as I'd wish. So What am I studying then? Well, it's Computer Science in Engineering at AGH University in Cracow. These studies are some sort of my backup, if I couldn't find a job in the future as an artist; but even then I would aim for a tech-artist role or anything related to art. The truth is that I still don't know what exactly I want to do in the future and that's because I’m passionate about so many things... traditional drawing, digital painting, 3D modeling, graphic design, computer graphics, game development, comics and so on. Besides I'm crazy about art and stuff, sometimes I go on a hike in the mountains with my group of friends, which is also fun. That's pretty much all about me.
