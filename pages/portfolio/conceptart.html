CONCEPT ART

THE WITCHER 3: LAST SACRIFICE

Elissa & Jagoda transformation

This design explores the transformation of one character, Elissa, into Jagoda, following a traumatic event. Once a noble heir to a powerful stronghold, Elissa was intelligent, ambitious, and drawn to forbidden blood magic. Her elegant costume—black and red robes, high collars, and gold jewelry—reflects her authority and cold charisma.
After a peasant uprising and the burning of her castle, Elissa is left disfigured, unconscious, and stripped of her magical artifact. She awakens in the wilderness with no memory of her past. Now known as Jagoda, she appears as a scarred, rugged woman in a servant’s dress—earthy, practical, and wounded, but still strangely skilled in herbs, leadership, and combat.
The concept illustrates a visual and narrative contrast between power and loss, identity and reinvention. It includes design variations, expression sheets, and a progression from aristocrat to outcast.

Created in: Photoshop
Project: The Witcher 3: Last Sacrifice

MOONEYE

Mooneye headshot

"Mooneye" is a character designed for a futuristic world where advanced technology coexists with ancient magic. Aria Nocturne is a mysterious bounty hunter raised in the slums after being abandoned by her parents as a child. Driven by bitterness and a desire for truth, she searches for answers about her origins—unaware that her DNA carries traces of a magical lineage. Her dormant powers can only awaken with the help of a special catalyst.
The aim of this project was to design a series of visually distinct and easily recognizable costume variants for a comic book or game setting. Each outfit reflects a different stage in Aria’s journey—ranging from stealth missions to combat-heavy scenarios—while staying true to her core identity.

Created in: Photoshop
Project: Mooneye
